About

BucHelp

BucHelp is a student created web-aplication designed to allow anyone to ask questions pertaining to East Tennessee State University. Similar to StackOverflow, BucHelp allows students, professors, staff, and visitors to ask for and give help to anything related to the ETSU. Users can ask new questions or answer existing questions to further our understanding and better our school. BucHelp was created for CSCI-4350-002 in a semester long scrum project.
